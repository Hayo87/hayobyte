Your problem

Legacy software Your business depends on IT—but legacy systems are holding you back.Outdated, undocumented, and built on obsolete technologies, these systems are expensive to maintain and risky to change. They block innovation, inflate IT costs, and make digital transformation harder than it should be. Modernizing them feels overwhelming. That’s where we come in!

Our Solution

We use automata learning to unlock the hidden behavior of your legacy systems—without touching a single line of code. By observing how your system responds to different inputs, we automatically build a model of its behavior. This lets us create a modern, scalable replacement that behaves just like the original—but without the baggage. Instead of months of manual analysis, we deliver results through:

  • Behavior Monitoring – We watch how your system responds to real inputs.
  • Model Inference – We reverse-engineer the business logic into a clean, structured model.
  • Modern Rebuild – We use that model to generate a reliable, maintainable system.
  • Validation & Deployment – We ensure the new version behaves just like the old one.
  • Ready for the Future – The new system can now evolve with your business goals.
